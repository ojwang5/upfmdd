About Us

Learn about the Uganda Police Force Music, Dance and Drama Department, our mission, vision, and branches across the country.

Department Overview

The Music, Dance and Drama Department of the Uganda Police Force was established in 1945 as a ceremonial unit by a british millitary officer from the Royal band named TEDDY BEAR. Over the years, it has evolved into a multifaceted cultural institution that not only serves the ceremonial needs of the police force but also represents Uganda's rich cultural heritage on national and international platforms.

Today, our department comprises over 300 talented musicians, dancers, and performers who are dedicated to preserving and promoting Uganda's diverse cultural expressions while fulfilling our duties to the nation.

Our Mission

To preserve, promote, and present Uganda's cultural heritage through excellence in music, dance, and drama while serving the Uganda Police Force and the nation with pride and distinction.

To collaborate with the community for prior community policing..

Our Vision

To be a world-class music institution that inspires national unity, preserves cultural heritage, and showcases Uganda's artistic excellence globally while maintaining the highest standards of professionalism in service.

Our Services

Discover the wide range of classic musical and cultural services offered by the Uganda Police Force Music, Dance and Drama Department.

Brass Band

Our internationally recognized brass band performs at state functions, national ceremonies, public and private events. With over 40 skilled musicians, we offer a repertoire ranging from military marches to contemporary Ugandan compositions.

Learn More

Jazz Band

Our jazz ensemble brings a sophisticated musical experience to diplomatic functions, corporate events, and cultural festivals. The band specializes in African jazz, traditional Ugandan rhythms with jazz influences, and international standards.

Learn More

Cultural Troupe

Our cultural troupe showcases Uganda's diverse ethnic traditions through authentic dance, music, and dramatic performances. The troupe performs traditional dances from all regions of Uganda, preserving and promoting our rich cultural heritage.

Learn More

Music Training

We offer comprehensive music education programs for police officers, schools, and community members. Our courses cover instrumental training, music theory, vocal techniques, and traditional Ugandan music, taught by experienced instructors. our musci syllabus is from the Associated Royal Schools Of Music(ABRSM)

Learn More

Instrument Repair

Our specialized workshop provides professional repair and maintenance services for brass, woodwind, string, and percussion instruments. Our technicians are trained to handle both Western and traditional African instruments with precision and care.

Learn More

Choir

Our award-winning choir performs a diverse repertoire including traditional Ugandan songs, African spirituals, and international choral works. The choir has represented Uganda at international festivals and performs regularly at national events.

Learn More
